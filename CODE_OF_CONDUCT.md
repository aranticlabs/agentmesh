# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to communicate with respect, assume good intent, and focus discussion on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, doxxing, sustained disruption, and any conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or exclusionary.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this standard.

Concerns can be reported to the project maintainers through the repository's normal contact channels.
